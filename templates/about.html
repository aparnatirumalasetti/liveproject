CMR Model High school was started in 1999 by "C Malla Reddy Educational Society (registered)".The founder of which was Late Sri C Malla Reddy. Whose vision was to bring quality education to all children, far and wide, privileged or under-privileged.CMR Model High School was proposed to be a model by itself for all round development of personality of the students at the crucial and formative stage.

Most of us go through our lives without any goal or purpose. Mr Ch. Malla Reddy knew from the beginning what he was born to do.
